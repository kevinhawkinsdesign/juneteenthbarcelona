Shipping, Returns & Refunds

Every item in our shop is printed on demand just for you and fulfilled by our print partner, Printful. Please read these policies before ordering.

Shipping & fulfillment

  • Orders are made to order, then printed and shipped by Printful. Typical production time is 2–7 business days, plus delivery time depending on your destination.
  • Estimated delivery within the EU is usually 3–8 business days after fulfillment; other regions may take longer.
  • Shipping is calculated at checkout based on your address and items. You'll receive an email confirmation, and tracking where available.

Returns & exchanges

  • Because each product is made to order, we can't accept returns or exchanges for the wrong size or a change of mind — please check the size chart on each product page before ordering.
  • We will gladly arrange a replacement or refund for items that arrive damaged, defective, or misprinted. Submit a claim within 30 days of receiving your order, with clear photos of the issue.
  • For packages lost in transit, contact us within 30 days of the estimated delivery date and we'll make it right.

Refunds

  • Approved refunds for damaged, defective, wrong, or lost items are issued to your original payment method via Stripe, typically within 5–10 business days of approval.
  • Refunds are not offered for buyer's remorse or an incorrect size selected on made-to-order items.

Support & contact

  • Questions about an order, sizing, or a problem with your item? Contact the Juneteenth Barcelona team — we're happy to help.
  • Our products are produced and shipped by our fulfillment partner, Printful. For fulfillment details you can also reach Printful's support at printful.com/contact / support@printful.com, or the Printful Help Center.
