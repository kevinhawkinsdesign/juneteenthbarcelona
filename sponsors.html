The Opportunity

An experience that's both delicious and culturally enriching.

Juneteenth Barcelona 2026 is a full-day celebration bringing together the African diaspora and allies from across Barcelona and beyond. International culture exchange through food, music, and collective joy.

Sponsoring this event places your brand at the heart of a growing, engaged community — with authentic exposure across digital, print, and on-site touchpoints before, during, and after the event.

What's on offer
  • Networking Meet the diaspora from Barcelona and surrounding areas — Afro-Spaniards, Black Americans, and people of color from everywhere.
  • Community Cookout A world food fair with traditional dishes from African countries, the Caribbean, local dishes, and more.
  • Cultural Immersion Live speakers, entertainers, and sponsors sharing insights, stories, and talent with the crowd.
  • Live Music Special guest DJs, live musicians, and performances throughout the day.
