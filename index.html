What Is Juneteenth

A Day of
Liberation
& Heritage

Juneteenth commemorates June 19, 1865 — the day enslaved people in Texas learned they were free, two and a half years after the Emancipation Proclamation.

In Barcelona, we honor this history, celebrate Black culture, and build bridges across the African diaspora living in and visiting the city.
