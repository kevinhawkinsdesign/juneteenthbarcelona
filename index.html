What Is Juneteenth

A Day of
Liberation
& Heritage

Juneteenth commemorates June 19, 1865 — the day enslaved people in Texas learned they were free, two and a half years after the Emancipation Proclamation.

In Barcelona, we honor this history, celebrate Black culture, and build bridges across the African diaspora living in and visiting the city.

Black History
Walking Tour

Trace the visible and invisible footprints of Black diasporic presence through Barcelona — from medieval accounts of African residents, to monuments funded by colonial profits, to the hubs of knowledge open today. A 2–3 hour self-guided walk through the city's hidden history.
