What Is Juneteenth

A Day of
Diaspora Unity
& Joy

At Juneteenth Barcelona, we believe that the African diaspora deserves a space to gather, celebrate, and be seen. Our mission is to honor the history of Black liberation & creativity while building a living, breathing community in one of Europe's most vibrant cities.

We honor history by celebrating Black culture, and building bridges across the African diaspora living in and visiting the city.
