BJuneteenth
May Meetup

Date Tuesday, May 19, 2026
Time 7:30 PM – 10:30 PM
Where Volta Bar · Hotel Borneta · Passeig de Picasso 26–30, El Born

Join us for the monthly BJuneteenth community meet-up, a gathering bringing together members of the African diaspora and allies as we begin preparing for Juneteenth Barcelona 2026. This meetup is about connection, collaboration, and building momentum together. The evening starts as a relaxed networking space where people can meet, exchange ideas, and learn more about the vision for Juneteenth Barcelona.
