Organising Entities

Juneteenth Barcelona is jointly organised by two legal entities:

Asociación Cultural Spanish Harlem BCN

Asociación Cultural Spanish Harlem BCN is a cultural association registered in Spain under registry number 73768, established on 4 September 2023, and based in Barcelona, Barcelonès region.

The association is dedicated to the dissemination of musical culture, organising events that serve as a meeting point for professionals, artists, and fans of live music. It promotes spaces for leisure, artistic performance, dance, and social and intercultural participation, and works for the recognition and labour regulation of musicians and artists at a national and international level.
